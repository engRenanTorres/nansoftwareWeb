Administrative and Financial Management Software

VETOR is a consultancy company that brings together in its technical staff professionals with extensive experience working with civil society organizations, international cooperation organizations, religious organizations, social movements, networks, foundations, and international donors (non-governmental and governmental).

Since its foundation in 2006, VETOR has worked on topics such as sustainable development, environment and climate, populations indigenous and traditional, community and territorial support funds, human rights, gender, and the right to food.

Through the use of technology, combined with the experience acquired in the operational sector, as well as in the administrative and financial sector, the VETOR reaffirms its mission to make use of the knowledge achieved to contribute to organizations of public interest, whether governmental or private, aiming to improve the quality of their intervention and, therefore, its degree of sustainability.

  • Type: Web Full stack application
  • Langages: HTML, CSS, JS
  • Frameworks: Express.js, JQuery
  • Country: Brazil
  • Live URL: https://www.consultoriavetor.com/

Designed to improve people knowledge

The first app to create free simulated engineering competition tests. The goal is to help engineers study for public exams. It has a prototype on the Play Store, which has reached about 500 users.

The system uses a MySQL database with a Nest.js backend and a React.js (Vite) web frontend, where the questions are managed. It will also have a web page for simulated tests and a new mobile app in the future.

  • Type: Full Stack
  • Langages: Typescript, HTML, CSS, JS
  • Plataforms: Web, Android
  • Frameworks: Nest.js, React.js, React Native
  • Live URL: https://engenhariadeconcursos.com.br/
  • App: Adroid app
